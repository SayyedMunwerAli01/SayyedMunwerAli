The 99 Names of Allah

Reflecting on the Most Beautiful Names (Al-Asma al-Husna)

The Quran refers to the Most Beautiful Names of God (al-asmāʼ al-ḥusná). Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said, "Allah has ninety-nine names, and whoever knows them will go to Paradise."
