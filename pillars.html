The Five Pillars of Islam

The fundamental practices that strengthen one's faith and structure a Muslim's life.

Shahada (Faith)

The declaration of faith: "There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is His messenger."

Learn More
Salah (Prayer)

The five daily prayers performed to maintain a direct connection with Allah.

Learn More
Zakat (Charity)

The practice of obligatory charity to purify wealth and support those in need.

Learn More
Sawm (Fasting)

The act of fasting during the holy month of Ramadan to develop self-control and empathy.

Learn More
Hajj (Pilgrimage)

The sacred pilgrimage to Mecca, required once in a lifetime for those who are able.
